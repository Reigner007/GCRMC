A Message from Leadership

Apstl. Chap. Dr. (Gen) George Chukwuma John

International President & Grand Commander General, ICRMC

Motto "Put on the whole armour of God" — Ephesians 6:10–20
HQ 2/4 Tom Hendrix Okogie Avenue, Off Suberu Oje Road, Casso B/Stop, Alagbado, Lagos, Nigeria
Phone +234 803 405 0342 (WhatsApp)  ·  +234 913 646 6842

God bless you beloved in Jesus Name. The International Christian Rescue Missionary Chaplain (ICRMC) is a Christian paramilitary organisation whose membership is open to Christians from all denominations. Our International Headquarters is located in Lagos, Nigeria.

Our Mission is to be an international institution of learning with the highest standard in preaching and teaching the Word of God (Matthew 28:19–20). We establish and maintain a place for interdenominational worship of the one true God, the Lord Jesus Christ. Our activities span charitable purposes, fostering unity and love, supporting governments, promoting peace, rescuing those in need, training chaplains, and promoting excellence, reverence, discipline and self-respect among members.

As a Christian paramilitary organisation, ICRMC is currently raising Commands across different continents and countries, recruiting officers to fill such commands. We believe strongly that ICRMC can work with churches, ministries, and denominations ready to preach the truth — to depopulate the kingdom of darkness and populate the Kingdom of God. We also extend open hands to individuals, families, organisations, and countries ready to help the needy.

By the grace of God, I previously served with the International Christian School of Chaplaincy / Christian Interdenominational Intervention Chaplain (ICSC/CIIC), rising through various ranks to Major General before honourably resigning under God's leadership. I served as the first Lagos 3 Commandant, sat on several committees, and concluded my service as SSA on Mobilisation/Expansion.

I remain a member of the Nigerian Ex-Servicemen (Nigeria Legionnaire Commission), holding the rank of Captain, and serve as a Patron in the Nigeria Legion All Auxiliary Arms, Lagos State Council Unit 47. I am an honourable member of the Nigerian Navy Officers' Mess, Ogun State.

As a Servant of God, I am an Apostle by calling and by His grace a Chief Apostle. I serve as Spiritual Director of the George Chukwuma John Apostolic Outreach — a non-denominational Evangelical Ministry of Reconciliation and Restoration headquartered in Wasimi, Ogun State — and as General Overseer of Final Exodus Bride Tabernacle, headquartered in Lagos State, Nigeria.

Thanks and remain blessed.
Apstl. Dr. Gen. George Chukwuma John

About Us

Who We Are

✦

Our Mission

ICRMC exists to rescue believers from the bondage of religious fragmentation, raising chaplains who proclaim the undivided gospel of Jesus Christ across every nation, institution, and social frontier.

◎

Our Vision

A global community of chaplains united beyond denominational walls — serving hospitals, schools, prisons, military, and communities with one gospel, one spirit, and one purpose.

❋

Our Values

Selfless service, revelation-based ministry, unity in diversity, integrity in calling, and unwavering commitment to the great commission of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Purpose

Aims & Objectives

01

Overcome Denominational Division

Bridge the gaps caused by selfish ambition and denominational pride, calling all ministers back to the singular gospel of Christ.

02

Establish Revelation-Based Ministry

Train and equip chaplains through deep study of God's Word — combating ignorance and spiritual fragmentation with sound biblical revelation.

03

Mobilise Global Chaplains

Deploy trained chaplains into hospitals, military, schools, prisons, and communities across nations.

04

Curb Breakaway Spirit

Address the inordinate ambition that drives ministers to start churches without divine calling — fostering accountability and godly submission.

05

Register Denominations

Welcome and affiliate Christian denominations of all traditions under the ICRMC umbrella, promoting cooperation without compromise.
